How it works?

  • Step 1

    Connect with Us
    The journey begins with a simple step - reaching out to us. You can either fill out request form or book a call directly. We value your time and preference.

  • Step 2

    Define the Scope
    Once we connect, we engage in a collaborative discussion to define the scope of your project. Your ideas, preferences, and vision guide this crucial step.

  • Step 3

    Crafting a Quote
    Based on our agreed-upon project scope, we create a detailed quote. Our transparent and itemized proposal ensures you understand the breakdown of the project costs.

  • Step 4

    Agreement and Payment
    When you're satisfied with your proposal, we move to the agreement phase. Once the agreement is in place, you send the payment, marking the official start of your project.

  • Step 5

    Commencement
    With payment received, our team swings into action. We kickstart the creative process, ensuring your vision takes shape in the initial draft.

  • Step 6

    Initial draft within 5 days
    Your time is valuable, so within just 5 days, we present you with the initial draft. This gives an early glimpse of the design direction we're heading.

  • Step 7

    Feedback and Alignment
    Your feedback is crucial. We engage in rounds of refinement and alignment to ensure the design aligns perfectly with your expectations.

  • Step 8

    Completion and Delivery
    Once the design reaches its final form, we deliver the completed project to you. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we're committed to ensuring the end result surpasses your expectations.
